Discover the Heart of North Africa

Tunisia is a North African country rich in history, culture, and natural beauty. From the Mediterranean coast to the vast Sahara desert, Tunisia offers diverse experiences.

Location

Tunisia is located in North Africa, bordered by Algeria and Libya, and has a beautiful coastline along the Mediterranean Sea.

History

It was once home to ancient Carthage and later became a part of the Roman Empire. The country has many UNESCO World Heritage Sites.

People & Culture

The Tunisian people are known for their hospitality. Arabic is the official language, and Islam is the main religion.
